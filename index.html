India is home to several holy shrines and religious destinations of different faiths including
Sikhism, Christianity, Hinduism, Jainism, Islam, Buddhism and more. Several religions
have their roots in India. You can go through the packages catering to religious tours in
India and choose the one that best meets your requirements.

Kedarnath

Jagannath Temple

Hemkund Sahib

The Pandavas were supposed to have pleased
Shiva by doing penance in Kedarnath. The temple
is one of the four major sites in India's Chota Char
Dham pilgrimage of Northern Himalayas.

An important pilgrimage in India, Jagannath
Temple is the finest monument in Odisha. The
main deities presiding over the temple are Lord
Jagannath, Balabhadra and Goddess Subhadra.

Situated in one of the most scenic locations in the
country, Hemkund Sahib adds charm to religious
tours in India. Unlike other Sikh pilgrimages in
India, Hemkund Sahib has a pentagonal structure.
